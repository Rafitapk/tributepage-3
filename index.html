JOHN LENNON

an English musician who gained worldwide fame

Here's a time line of John Lennon :

  • 1940 John Lennon was born in Liverpool to Julia Stanley and Alfred Lennon during a blitzkrieg attack.
  • 1956 Julia buys John a mail-order guitar. He subsequently forms his first band, the Quarrymen.
  • 1957 John asks Paul McCartney to join the Quarrymen while playing at a church fair in Liverpool.
  • 1958 John writes "Hello Little Girl. " his first song
  • 1961 The Beatles, with John Lennon as front man, debut at Liverpool's Cavern Club.
  • 1962 John marries Cynthia Powell.
  • 1963 The Beatles record the Please Please Me album in one day.
  • 1964 The Beatles begin their first American tour, playing on The Ed Sullivan Show and at The Coliseum in Washington, D.C. and Carnegie Hall in New York City.
  • 1965 John composes "Help!"
  • 1966 John films How I Won the War, directed by Richard Lester, director of A Hard Day's Night. He writes "Strawberry Fields" during the filming.
  • 1968 Apple Corps, Ltd. begins business as the Beatles attempt to control their own artistic pursuits and offer relative unknowns the chance to submit artistic projects of any kind.
  • 1968 John and Yoko are arrested for possession of marijuana.
  • 1968 John and Cynthia are divorced.
  • 1969 John and Yoko are married on Gibraltar.
  • 1973 John and Yoko buy an apartment in the Dakota Building on Central Park West and W. 72nd St.
  • 1974 John makes his final concert appearance at Madison Square Garden with Elton John.
  • 1980 John and Yoko returned to the studio to work on double fantasy plotting a full-fledged comeback. Then, shockingly, Lennon was shot to death outside the building where he and Ono lived on December 8, 1980.
